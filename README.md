# Adaptive-Study-Schedule-Optimization-Using-Multi-Constraint-Graph-Modeling-and-Heuristic-Algorithms

Student often struggle to decide what to study, when or for how long, especially right before exams. As they have many subjects to cover and limited time.
Existing study planner and timetables apps or websites usually provide static schedules and do not adapt to the syllabus weightage, difficulty of the topics, and deadlines

To design and implement an intelligent study planner that uses graph-based time optimization to automatically generate a personalized and conflict-free study timetable, considering topic priorities, deadlines, and limited available study hours.
